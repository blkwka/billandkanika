insuLIN

insuLIN" is Kanika Madhok and Bill Odiwuor Kawaka's vision of an educational yet fun virtual pet game where players take care of LIN and manage her hunger, blood sugar, and happiness. This game is our response to the scarcity and fear that new people and children face regarding type 1 diabetes. We aim to provide a free interactive service to those who are unaware of daily living with T1D. Our intention is to continue this journey by updating the game to better inform people. Additionally, we aim to gather insights into consumer trends and suggestions, which are crucial for managing any company, business, nonprofit, or group.
